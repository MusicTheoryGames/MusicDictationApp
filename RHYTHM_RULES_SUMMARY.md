# RHYTHM RULES SUMMARY
## Comprehensive Constraints for Educational Melodic Dictation

### 🎵 **Simple Time (4/4 and 3/4)**

#### **Simple Rhythms:**
- **Allowed**: Whole notes, half notes, dotted half notes, quarter notes
- **Patterns**: Basic combinations focusing on quarter and half note relationships

#### **Complex Rhythms:**
- **Allowed**: All simple rhythms + dotted quarter notes + eighth notes
- **Grouping**: Eighth notes must be **paired** (no isolated eighths)
- **Beaming**: Maximum 4 consecutive eighth notes

#### **Measure Restrictions:**
| Measure Count | Whole Notes | Half Notes (per measure) |
|---------------|-------------|-------------------------|
| 2-measure     | ❌ None     | 📝 Max 1               |
| 4-measure     | 📝 Max 1 total | 📝 Max 2           |
| 8-measure     | 📝 Max 1 total | 📝 Max 2           |

### 🎵 **Compound Time (6/8 and 9/8)**

#### **Simple Rhythms:**
- **Allowed**: Dotted half notes, dotted quarter notes, quarter notes, eighth notes, groups of 3 eighths
- **Focus**: Compound beat feeling (groups of 3)

#### **Complex Rhythms:**
- **Allowed**: All simple rhythms + eighth-sixteenth-eighth patterns + eighth-quarter patterns
- **Grouping**: Eighth notes in **groups of 3** or compound subdivisions
- **Subdivisions**: Sixteenth notes only in **8-16-8** patterns

#### **Measure Restrictions:**
| Measure Count | Dotted Half Notes |
|---------------|-------------------|
| 2-measure     | ❌ None          |
| 4-measure     | 📝 Max 2 (6/8), Max 1 (9/8) |
| 8-measure     | 📝 Max 4 (6/8), Max 2 (9/8) |

## 🔧 **Validation System Features**

### **Automatic Checking:**
- ✅ **Beat count accuracy** (prevents mathematical errors)
- ✅ **Allowed rhythm verification** (ensures only approved rhythms)
- ✅ **Measure restriction enforcement** (whole/half note limits)
- ✅ **Grouping rule validation** (proper eighth note pairing/grouping)
- ✅ **Overall distribution analysis** (rhythm variety recommendations)

### **Error Prevention:**
- 🚫 **Isolated eighth notes** in simple time
- 🚫 **Improper groupings** in compound time  
- 🚫 **Excessive long notes** in short examples
- 🚫 **Invalid subdivision patterns**

### **Educational Guidelines:**
- 📚 **Progressive complexity** (simple → complex rhythms)
- 📚 **Style-appropriate patterns** (Classical period practices)
- 📚 **Listening skill development** (appropriate difficulty progression)

## 🎯 **Educational Benefits**

### **Simple Rhythms:**
- **Focus on pitch discrimination** without rhythm complexity
- **Clear beat patterns** for beginning ear training
- **Classical melodic flow** with appropriate note values

### **Complex Rhythms:**
- **Rhythm + pitch challenges** for advanced students
- **Subdivision awareness** in compound time
- **Authentic Classical patterns** for style recognition

### **Progressive Difficulty:**
- **2-measure**: Basic patterns, limited long notes
- **4-measure**: More variety, some long notes allowed
- **8-measure**: Full vocabulary with structural considerations
